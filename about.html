About Farm Produce Tracker

Empowering Namibian farmers with digital solutions for efficient farm management and increased productivity.

Our Mission

Agriculture is the backbone of Namibia's economy and livelihood. Our Farm Produce & Livestock Tracker aims to digitalize farm management, providing small and medium-scale farmers with accessible, easy-to-use solutions for managing produce and livestock effectively.

Our Goal

To eliminate paper-based systems and memory-dependent record keeping that often lead to inefficiency, data loss, and reduced profits.

Target Users

Small and medium-scale farmers, agricultural cooperatives, farm managers, and buyers across Namibia.

Expected Impact

Improved record keeping, reduced losses from mismanagement, and better opportunities for farmers to connect with buyers.

Key Features

Farmer Registration

Secure authentication system for farmers to create accounts and manage their farm data.

Crop Management

Track crops like maize and mahangu, monitor harvest weights, and analyze seasonal trends.

Livestock Tracking

Manage cattle, goats, chickens and other livestock with comprehensive inventory tracking.

Sales Analytics

Record sales transactions and generate reports to track profitability and trends.

Mobile Friendly

Responsive design that works seamlessly on desktop and mobile browsers.

Secure & Reliable

Robust security measures and reliable data storage for your farm information.
